Labour and Immigration Law

MALOSHA ESQUIRE provides legal services to both companies and individuals on employment, labour and immigration issues. Whereas our clients enjoys other services including opinion on compliance of employment laws and regulations, terminal benefits and pensions schemes, collective bargains and agreements, and so forth. Our services in this area are structured to make sure that there is a minimum delay in meeting the needs of both employers and employees so as to allow the production.
Our Law Office has also all the requisite knowhow and resources that support our clients’ immigration needs. We cater their common need as regards the status of their employees, experts and their respective families. Akin to the employment law practice, in ensuring its clients’ immigration status, we are actually a one stop centre for all legal needs of its clients. MALOSHA ESQUIRE therefore provides immigration services to clients such as; work and resident permits for clients, employees and respective families, investment incentives for foreigners and other residential status needs of the clients.

We provide legal services and facilitate in establishing real estate project companies, conveyancing, sales, mortgage, leasing, charging, and property management, including participating in negotiation, drafting contracts, providing legal opinions, acting as legal witnesses and other relevant business. Our Law Office has an inquiry office rendering such systematic non-litigation services as real estate credibility investigation (due diligence), negotiation and signing of agreements, transfer of property, and guaranteeing the property purchaser`s interest in all phases of the deal.
MALOSHA ESQUIRE is aware that employment, labor and benefits issues are complex and challenging for every business — more so when operations cross borders and cultures as part of a multinational presence. Steering safely through complicated laws and regulations in home markets and abroad is critical to maintaining a competitive advantage.
