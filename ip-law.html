Intellectual Property Law

Intelectual Property Law

This is ever growing area of business law which seeks to protect the rights of the innovators. At MALOSHA ESQUIRE, we also seek to provide the rights of our business clients in protecting their intellectual properties. We deal with trade and service marks registration and renewal, unlawful interference with properties, conveyances, mortgages, licensing and leasing arrangements, and so forth. We will take care of your innovations when you are doing business.

We facilitate registration of trade and service marks and patents, confirmation and protection of trade and service marks, patents, copyright, consultation, negotiation and contract drafting concerning intellectual property trade, transfer, permit and registration.
